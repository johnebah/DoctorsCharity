DOCTORS CHARITY

At Doctors Charity we provide compassionate and professional home care services for individuals with limited mobility or those unable to access healthcare facilities. Our dedicated team of medical professionals and caregivers deliver personalized, high quality care in the comfort of patient”s homes, ensuring they receive medical attention,support and assistance they need to maintain their health and wellbeing

Join Us Via Telemedicine

Doctors Charity uses information technology to connect qualified healthcare workers, who wish to volunteer their time and skills to alleviate the health issues that plague the continent of Africa, with the people who need such services. For these volunteers who are unable to leave their jobs to travel for onsite medical intervention, Telemedicine offers the perfect avenue for them to do so. Working with their free time and schedule, Doctors Charity can schedule consultation appointments with them and patients in Africa, thereby efficiently meeting the same goals. Ihotu Telemedicine Clinic in Nigeria is a major partner in this regard.
