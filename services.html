Our Services

At doctors charity , we are committed to breaking barriers in healthcare access through our medical services. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals provides comprehensive home visits,ensuring that individuals with limited mobility or other challenges receive high- quality medical care in the comfort of their own home. In addition to home care services, we extend our impact globally through medical missions abroad, bringing essential healthcare to undeserved communities around the world. Our goal is to ensure that no one is left behind when it comes to receiving the medical attention they deserve.

Home Care

At Doctors Charity, we provide compassionate and professional home care for individuals who need medical assistance in the comfort of their own home. Our team of skilled nurses and caregivers offers personalized care plans to support recovery, manage chronic conditions, and promote overall well-being. Whether it's post-surgery recovery or ongoing care for a loved one, we ensure the highest standards of medical attention and comfort in a familiar environment.

Equipment Distribution

Doctors charity provides carefully soourced medical supplies such as wheelchairs, mobility aids and home care essentials to economically disadvantaged individuals and healthcarefacilities struggling with resource limitations. Through our equipment distribution program we aim to bridge the healthcare gap empowering patients too manage their health with dignity and independence.

Pro Bono Services

Committed to making healthcare accessible, our team of volunteer doctors offers free medical consultations, treatments, and minor procedures to underserved communities. We believe that quality healthcare should be a right, not a privilege.
